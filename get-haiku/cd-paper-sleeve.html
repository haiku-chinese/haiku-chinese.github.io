Haiku DVD Paper Sleeve

Do you want to hand out Haiku Installation DVDs to friends and colleagues? To help distinguish it from all the other DVDs that litter people's desks, we have a nice little DVD paper sleeve you can print out and fold yourself.
Haiku DVD paper sleeve: US letter size (73 KiB PDF)

Use this WonderBrush file (903 KiB) as a template for your personal sleeves. Please respect the Haiku trademarks, if you plan to distibute those sleeves.

All you have to do is get the above PDF file, print it and follow the instructions:

Step 1

Print out the PDF, making sure it's printed at 100%, not "fit to page" or any such setting.
100-120 g/m2 should give a nice solid feel to the cover. Heavier paper gets difficult to fold.

Step 2

Note: The folding lines are always on the outside of an edge.
Lay the sheet face down and fold up both sides lengthwise.

Step 3

Fold up the future back of the cover.

Step 4

Expand the wings back out.

Step 5

Fold the wings back in like this. Crease the edges very well.

Step 6

Fold up the flap and fold the corners like earmarks.

Step 7

Put in your Haiku Install DVD.

Step 8

Fold in the closing flap.

That's it, you are done. Now, go and start handing out those Haiku DVDs!
